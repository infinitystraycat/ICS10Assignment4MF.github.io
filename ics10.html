02/07/2024

Today I cooked food with my friends in the dorms. We made Japanese curry, using ingredients that we bought at Mitsuwa.

Ingredients

1) 1 Carrot
2) 2 Potatoes
3) 1 Onion
4) 1 Pound Chicken
5) 1 Box of curry roux
6) 3 Cups of rice
7) Few tablespoons of oil

Instructions

1) Wash all vegetables until they are clean
2) Cut your carrots, potatoes, onions, and chicken into bite sized pieces
3) Put your pot onto the stove, and heat it until it's hot
4) Put the oil into the pot, and make sure that the oil is hot enough
5) Put your cut up vegetables and meat into the pot and stir until cooked
6) Put water into the pot according to the instructions on your curry box
7) On the side, put 3 cups of rice and 3.5 cups of water into a rice cooker and cook
8) Once the water in the pot has boiled, put in your cubes of curry roux into the water
9) Stir until the pot thickens
10) Put rice into a bowl, and put your curry ontop of the rice
11) Enjoy!

Today I cooked food alone in the dorms. I made korean bossam, a boiled pork dish.

Ingredients

1) 1 lb pork belly or pork shoulder
2) 1 onion
3) 1/2 of a cabbage or lettuce
4) 2 tbsp garlic
5) 2 tbsp ginger

6) 2 tbsp gochujang
7) 2 tbsp miso
8) 1 tbsp honey
9) 1 tbsp garlic
10) 1 sesame oil

Instructions

1) Boil a pot of water, and place the pork into the water
2) Wash and slice the onions
3) Remove the excess things that come up when you boil the pork, and throw them away
4) Put the sliced onions, garlic, and ginger into the pot and boil 5) Once the broth tastes good and the pork is well cooked, take out the pork and put onto a cutting board.
6) Cut the pork into bite sized pieces
7) Wash your lettuce, and cut big, handsized pieces.
8) In a separate bowl, mix all of the ingredients in orange
9) On the lettuce, combine your sauce and pork. Add kimchi to taste.
10) Enjoy!
